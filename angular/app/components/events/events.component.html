ABOUT TECHNOSEARCH

TECHNOSEARCH'16 will be adding another milestone in the history of MANIT. Our technical festival boasts the status of Largest Technical event in Central India, and this year we'll be showcasing ourselves beyond the expectations of attendees. From extravagant events to lecture series from distinguished personalities, from highly learning workshops to various fun events, there will be no stone unturned. Along with these, exhibitions, gaming zone and techno cultural nights will be adding charm to the fest.
